I am a digital collections specialist with a background in cultural anthropology, based in Brooklyn, New York.

I draw inspiration from material culture and American art traditions as visual references for experiences of the environment and cultural belonging. I am currently completing my master’s degree in Museums & Digital Culture @ Pratt Info School , focusing on the evolving relationship between art, audiences, and digital experience.

I care for a Chihuahua/Dachshund mix named Thomas, and enjoy Ken Burns documentaries, reality television, and "stewarding" a collection vintage shopping finds.
